# StakeKE — SEO Strategy & Keyword Plan

## Competitor Analysis

### 1. Stake Kenya Sacco (stakekenyasacco.co.ke)
- A physical SACCO (Savings & Credit Co-operative) based in Migori County
- Targets: loans, savings, sacco membership
- Weakness: no online M-Pesa investment product; targets rural western Kenya
- They rank for "stake kenya" brand queries — we need to differentiate with "online", "M-Pesa", "invest", "ROI"

### 2. SaccoReview.co.ke — "Stake Kenya Sacco Goes Digital"
- Review/news article about the Migori SACCO going digital for loans
- This article ranks for "stake kenya digital" — it's editorial content, not a competitor product
- Opportunity: create more authoritative content to outrank this article on "stake kenya" queries

### 3. TopCasinoKenya.com — Stake.com Kenya Review
- Covers Stake.com (global crypto casino) for Kenyan players
- Completely different product (gambling, not investment)
- Risk: Google may conflate "stake kenya" with gambling — our content must clearly signal legitimate financial services

### 4. GhanaSoccerNet — Stake Kenya App Reviews
- App review aggregator, covering Stake.com Kenya
- Same gambling angle — not a direct competitor

### Competitor Gap Summary
| Gap | Opportunity |
|---|---|
| No competitor owns "M-Pesa investment platform Kenya" | Target this exact phrase |
| "stake kenya" dominated by SACCO and casino | Differentiate as "StakeKE" or "stake money Kenya online" |
| No competitor has an investment blog in English + Swahili | Blog content authority |
| Ziidi (Safaricom) targets formal savers | Target informal, mobile-first investors |
